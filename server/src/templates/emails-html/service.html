Service Details
Initial Diagnostic
Customer reported intermittent control system faults and unexpected emergency stops during high-speed operations. Diagnostic testing revealed sensor misalignment in the feed system and corrupted motion profiles in the servo controller memory.
Technician: Michael Chen
Date: May 8, 2025
Repairs Performed
  1. Recalibrated and aligned all feed system sensors
  2. Replaced faulty resolver in secondary servo motor
  3. Updated controller firmware to version 8.5.3
  4. Reinstalled factory motion profiles and validated all operational modes
  5. Performed full system diagnostic test through 500 cycles at varying speeds
Technician: Michael Chen, Amanda Roberts
Date: May 9, 2025
Recommended Preventive Action
Based on our diagnostic findings, we recommend scheduling a full system calibration every 6 months to prevent similar issues. Additionally, upgrading the servo drive cooling system would improve long-term reliability for your high-cycle production requirements.
